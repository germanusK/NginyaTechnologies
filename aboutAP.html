Nginya, the Akose(Bakossi language) translation of "Giant" was concieved by 6 young Cameroonians. Passionate about technology, they came up with this idea to improve on tech in their country and affect millions on a global scale. Founded in December 2018, we are specialized in the building of websites, web applications, android applications, desktop applications, providing networking solutions and graphic design.
